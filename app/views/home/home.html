Thought Process

Being a web developer and recently trying on the hat of stay at home dad I thought to myself There has to be a better way to manage the house. So I started applying some Agile Methodologies to the way my wife and I ran our house. So, after planning with my wife on what actually needs to be done since there was more to it than just magic, I began to put together a scrum type management tool for your household. We THINK it will work for us, give it a try and see if it works for you.



Promote family bonding with weekly household scrum meetings to plan tasks.


Get your kids, and yourselves, away from electronics and communicate with each other.


Instill accountability in each family member for tasks they are responsible for.
